Overview

Hub71 will host its annual event in the first quarter of 2023 to celebrate its achievements and impact in 2022. The event will take place in person for the first time since the pandemic, bringing together Hub71’s stakeholders across Abu Dhabi’s tech ecosystem. The event will involve founders, investors, strategic partners, and media for meaningful discussions on Abu Dhabi’s technology and innovation landscape and its role in strengthening economic resilience across key sectors.

Overview

Objective

  • Highlight Hub71's impact on the Abu Dhabi economy and regional tech ecosystem
  • Reinforce Abu Dhabi's position as a regional hub of choice for innovators and investors
  • Showcase the quality of Abu Dhabi's tech ecosystem through meaningful discussions

Key Messages

Hub71 is leading the growth of technology entrepreneurship to support Abu Dhabi’s economy

Hub71 is accelerating the development and growth of widely adopted technologies in the region

Hub71 is attracting leading partners and investors to enhance Abu Dhabi’s innovation ecosystem
